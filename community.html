EluciDATA Community - A facilitator and network for your AI innovation projects

The EluciDATA Community is initiated by the EluciDATA Lab of Sirris. It gathers a network of companies with an interest in data innovation and AI. It’s main objective is to provide a platform for knowledge transfer, networking and collaboration.     

BENEFITS

 

Actively participating in the EluciDATA Community provides you with access to:

Knowledge transfer

Gain insights in emerging technologies through comprehensible industrial use cases on how to successfully apply AI and data science techniques in several sectors

Community 

Preferred access to the two annual Community events:

  • EluciDATA Brokerage – “Data and AI driven innovation”: offering the opportunity for networking, learning about or pitching new R&D initiatives, forming new R&D partnerships and following keynote presentations on emerging technology trends
  • EluciDATA Tech Talk: addressing industrial deployment of data innovation and AI, showcasing recent success stories and realised proof-of-concepts on real-world use cases, and exchanging technological know-how and business insights with peers. Topics from the last editions include: "How to fully exploit the data generated by your assets?", Tthe relevance of data security and data privacy in an industrial context", "Are advanced AI methods ready to be applied in industry?". 

Network

Network with peers and discuss shared challenges, potential solutions and lessons learned 

Collaboration

Be informed about new R&D proposals and take part in their industrial consortia
