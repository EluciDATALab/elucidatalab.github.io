EluciDATA Lab involved in TRAIL (Trusted AI Labs) – Walloon initiative to foster research and collaboration around AI

Category > Blog

Fri 09 October 2020
Share This Post :

TRAIL is an overall structure, which aims at fostering collaboration and research around AI in Wallonia and Brussels between universities and research centers in line with regional policies. 

In the last months, the EluciDATA Lab has been involved in the definition of this partnership and its service offering. The Lab will mainly be involved in activities related to the adoption of AI by industry. 

For more information, we invite you to have a look at the website of the partnership: https://trail.ac
